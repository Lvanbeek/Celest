Celestial Guild Rank Roles

Not sure what you should be doing to be promoted? Are you doing the following?

Guild Master:

Manages and controls all guild functionality and leads the guild as lead representative.

Assistant Guild Master:

Assists the Guild Master and, in the absence of the Guild Master, inherits all powers and responsibilities of the Guild Master.

Officer:

Each officer has a different “niche” within the leadership:

  • Raid Leader: Officer who manages and leads the Core Team raids.

  • Lore Master: Officer who is well versed in the lore of the game.

  • Event Organizer: Officer who organizes non-raid events.

  • Public Relations Manager: Officer who manages the guild Discord, MoTD and other announcements.

Officer Alt:

Rank for alts of Officers.

Elite:

Members who have applied to be an officer and is currently proving that they truly wish to be an officer.

Raider:

Members who participate in the weekly Core Team raids.

Veteran:

Members who define our legacy as a guild.

Member:

Members with an established presence within the guild.

Alt:

Alts for Members, Veterans, Raiders, and Elites.

Recruit:

New members to the guild and still getting to know the guild
