Attendance


Remember to always let one for the raid officers (Hekus or Shammiebro) know if you cannot make it to any raid time at least 3 days in advance.


You can only miss one raid time a month -- unannounced --, you get a "strike" for each additional missed raid time that is not announced until you reach 3 strikes for that month. After which you will be put on probation from the raid team until further notice.


"strikes" will reset every full month you do not accumulate more.
